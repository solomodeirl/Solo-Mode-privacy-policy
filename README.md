## Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

### Types of Data Collected

#### Personal Data

While using our Service, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you. Personally identifiable information may include, but is not limited to:

- Email address
- Display name
- Profile image
- Usage Data

#### Usage Data

Usage Data is collected automatically when using the Service. Usage Data may include information such as your Device's Internet Protocol address (e.g., IP address), device type, operating system version, the pages of our Service that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages, unique device identifiers, and other diagnostic data.

#### Information Collected While Using the Application

While using our Application, in order to provide features of our Service, we may collect, with your prior permission:

- **Profile Information:** Your name, email address, and profile picture when you sign in via Google Sign-In. This information is used to create and manage your account within the app.
- **Calibration Data:** Information you provide during the onboarding calibration process, such as gender, age, height, weight, fitness level, dietary preferences, study habits, and wellness goals. This data is stored locally on your device and used to personalize your quests and stat calculations.
- **Quest and Progress Data:** Your quest completions, stat points, XP, streak data, rank progression, and daily logs. This data is stored locally on your device and optionally synced to Firebase for cloud backup.
- **Subscription Information:** Purchase history and subscription status for in-app purchases. This data is managed by Google Play or Apple App Store and is not stored on our servers.

You can enable or disable access to this information at any time through your Device settings.

### How to Delete Your Account and Data:

1. **Email us** at **contact.solomode.irl@gmail.com** with the subject line: **"Delete My Account"**.
